About SHIFA

SHIFA Community Based Mental Health aims to enhance community mental health by:

Establishing a community-based system for early identification and referral of Common Mental Disorders (CMDs) and Severe Mental Disorders (SMDs).

Building a supportive familial environment for improved mental health.

Strengthening universal, selective, and indicative prevention systems to promote mental health wellbeing, empowerment, and resilience in rural communities.

Ensuring entitlements for Persons with Disabilities (PWDs) and Persons with Severe Disabilities (PPSDs).

SHIFA Mental Health and Disability Project

As part of SHIFA-II, the Community mental Health & Disability project was launched in year 2012 in 35 Gram Panchayats of Sadholi Kadim Development Block, Saharanpur District, Uttar Pradesh.

The project is supported by TEAR Australia. The growing issue of broken relationship, difficulties to cope with fast raging development and social changes, increase dependency on drugs and alcohol, poverty, suicide and increase burden of psycho-somatic disorder pushed CHD to initiate long term Mental Health interventions.

Operational Scope:
Since 2012, the SHIFA Project has been active in 35 Gram Panchayats within the Sadholi Kadim blocks of Saharanpur District, Uttar Pradesh. Recently, it expanded its activities to include Muzaffarabad block.

Who are we

HCA

Herbertpur Christian Hospital (HCH) has been serving and caring for the poor and marginalized people for many decades. Founded in 1936 by a British medical missionary doctor, HCH is situated at the foothills of the Himalayas in Herbertpur town of Dehradun, Uttarakhand. HCH actively serves the communities around with its sole aim to provide affordable and quality health care for all people regardless of race, caste, creed or religion with primary focus on the poor and marginalized. It is 110-bed hospital that offers medical services in Medicine, General and minimally invasive surgery, Paediatric surgery, Paediatrics, Orthopaedics, Family Medicine, Obstetrics & amp; Gynaecology, Ophthalmology, Dentistry, Clinical Psychology and Counselling, Physio and Occupational Therapy

EHA

“Emmanuel Health Association” is a federal body comprised of 20-small Health Organisation with its Central Office located at Nehru Place- New Delhi. Emmanuel Hospital Association (EHA) was founded in 1970 as an indigenous Christian Health and Development agency serving the people of North. EHA treats more than 500,000 patients each year in some of India's most needy areas.

Our Approach

The project is rooted in a rights-based understanding of Disability and employs various community-led strategies to address the needs of individuals, particularly those with Psychosocial Disabilities.

Investing in Task-Shifting role by lay health worker to overcoming the gap of professional health worker.

Strengthening local potential through culturally appropriate means for People and families with lived experience to recognize that they are the “Experts of their Own Care”!

Building continuum of care through home based psychosocial support, project facilitate about the importance of treatment, mental health literacy, managing behavior, etc. And also explore the possibilities of establishing meaningful and gainful Livelihood connection.

Create Community Conscientization by addressing determinant of mental health by use of thematic based pictorial flash cards and other IEC tool.

Community organization and leadership development so that project ownership would be build built.
